Diversity in STEM

Lily Liu & Deanna Baris

In a society reliant on the constant development of new science and technology, diversity in STEM diversity is a pressing issue. At present, science and technology fields are laregely dominated by white men. These trends are visible throughout higher education

Why does this matter?

Studies have shown that diversity improves outcomes for group work.

In order to develop effective technologies, engineering populations should be representative of their user populations.
